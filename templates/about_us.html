A New Way For Spirituality.

Why use it:

Accessibility:

For those who cannot physically visit temples due to distance, illness, or other circumstances, Live Darshan provides a way to connect with their faith.

Convenience:

Devotees can participate in rituals and ceremonies from anywhere at any time, offering a flexible and accessible spiritual experience.

Enhanced Spirituality:

The ability to witness rituals firsthand can deepen spiritual connection and foster a sense of belonging to the religious community.

"Live Darshan" portal offers devotees the ability to witness temple rituals and ceremonies remotely through live streaming, providing a spiritual experience from the comfort of their homes. These portals often include information about festivals, events, and online offerings like prayers and aarti.
